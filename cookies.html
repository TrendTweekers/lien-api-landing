Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

LienDeadline uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • Authentication: Remembering that you're logged in
  • Session Management: Maintaining your session while using the calculator
  • Security: Protecting against fraud and abuse

Cannot be disabled: These cookies are essential and cannot be turned off without breaking core functionality.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks page views, user interactions, and website performance (anonymized)
  • Umami Analytics: Privacy-focused analytics that doesn't use cookies (uses localStorage instead)

Can be disabled: You can opt out of analytics cookies through your browser settings or by using browser extensions.

No Advertising or Tracking Cookies

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party marketing cookies. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies (may break website functionality)
  • Set preferences for third-party cookies

Browser-specific instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of Google Analytics:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
  • Or disable JavaScript in your browser (will break website functionality)

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

GDPR (EU Users)

Under GDPR, we must obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies. By using our website, you consent to our use of analytics cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

CCPA (California Users)

Under CCPA, you have the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information or use cookies for advertising purposes, so no opt-out is necessary.

Cookie Retention

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Authentication Cookies: Expire after 30 days of inactivity
  • Analytics Cookies: Retained for up to 2 years (Google Analytics default)

Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Analytics and performance tracking (see Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (see Stripe's Privacy Policy)

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Questions About Cookies?
