Is this legal advice?

No. LienDeadline is a calculation tool based on state statutes. Always consult a licensed attorney for legal guidance on filing mechanics liens.

What states are covered?

What's a mechanics lien?

A mechanics lien is a legal claim against a property that secures payment for labor or materials provided. If you're not paid, the lien can force a property sale to recover your money.

Our calculations are based on current state statutes and updated quarterly. However, laws can change. We recommend verifying critical deadlines with your attorney. Accuracy disclaimer: LienDeadline provides estimates and cannot be held liable for missed deadlines.
