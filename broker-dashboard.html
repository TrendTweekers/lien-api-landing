Broker Payouts & Commissions

Common questions about how commissions and payouts work

You choose one of two commission models:

  • $500 one-time per referred customer (paid once, after the hold period)
  • $50/month recurring per active referred customer (paid monthly, after the hold period for each payment)

The model is set per broker and applies to future referrals unless changed by admin.

Commissions are held for 60 days after the customer's payment date. After the hold period, eligible commissions move into "Due" and can be paid in the next payout batch.

Refunded/chargebacked payments do not become eligible for payout. If a subscription cancels or becomes past-due, recurring commissions stop for that customer until payments resume.

Brokers see:

  • Their referral list + status (active / pending / etc.)
  • Their payout totals (earned / on hold / due / paid) where available
  • Their chosen payout method details (e.g., PayPal / bank / Wise)

Admin-only:

  • Full payout ledger events, batch creation, and internal notes
  • Approvals/rejections and payout execution details
  • Transaction IDs / accounting exports

"On Hold" means the 60-day hold window hasn't passed yet. Once it passes, it becomes "Due" automatically.

Admin pays brokers in payout batches. Each batch contains one or more eligible (Due) commission events and is marked paid together with a timestamp and optional transaction reference.
