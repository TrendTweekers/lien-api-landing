Our Mission

Building material suppliers lose $1.2 billion per year by missing mechanics lien deadlines. LienDeadline exists to eliminate that loss.

We provide instant, accurate deadline calculations via web calculator and API, helping suppliers protect their right to payment without the complexity and cost of traditional solutions.

What We Do

  • • Instant Calculations: Calculate mechanics lien deadlines in 0.3 seconds
  • • API Integration: Integrate deadline calculations into your ERP/CRM system
  • • State Coverage: Support for 28 states covering 75% of the US construction market
  • • Affordable Pricing: $299/month per branch - 60% cheaper than alternatives
  • • Free Trial: 6 free calculations to get started (no credit card required)

Why We Exist

Traditional lien deadline solutions are expensive, slow, and require manual form filling. Building material suppliers need something faster, cheaper, and designed for high-volume operations.

LienDeadline was built specifically for material suppliers who process hundreds of invoices per day and can't afford to miss a single deadline.
