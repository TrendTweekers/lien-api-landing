About LienDeadline

Helping construction professionals protect their lien rights, one deadline at a time.

Our Mission

Construction companies lose millions of dollars every year to missed mechanics lien deadlines. A single missed deadline can forfeit $75,000+ in unpaid receivables—money that's rightfully owed but can never be recovered.

LienDeadline exists to eliminate this problem. We provide fast, accurate, and affordable mechanics lien deadline calculations so construction professionals can protect their receivables without breaking the bank.

Why We Built This

After seeing construction companies struggle with expensive, slow, and incomplete lien deadline calculators, we knew there had to be a better way. Existing solutions were either too expensive ($500+/month), too slow (taking minutes per calculation), or incomplete (covering only a fraction of US states).

We built LienDeadline to be the solution we wished existed: comprehensive coverage of all 50 states + DC, lightning-fast calculations (0.3 seconds), and affordable pricing ($299/month for unlimited access).

What Makes Us Different

Complete Coverage

All 50 US states + DC. No gaps, no exceptions. We handle complex states like Texas (month+day formulas), California (Notice of Completion triggers), and Hawaii (shortest 45-day deadline).

Lightning Fast

0.3 second calculation time. Our API averages 200-300ms response time. No waiting, no delays—get your deadlines instantly.

60% Cheaper

$299/month for unlimited calculations vs. $500+/month from competitors. Same accuracy, better value.

Developer-Friendly

RESTful API with clear documentation. Easy integration with QuickBooks, Procore, and custom ERPs.
