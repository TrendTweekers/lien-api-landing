Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025
Strictly Confidential

1. Introduction

LienDeadline.com ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mechanics lien deadline calculator and API services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (hashed), subscription status
  • Calculation Data: Invoice dates, states, project types, and calculation results
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Contact Information: Name, email, company when you contact support or request API access

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: API call counts, calculation history, feature usage
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps
  • Analytics: Google Analytics and Umami Analytics (anonymized usage patterns)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Provide lien deadline calculations, API access, and account management
  • Communication: Send welcome emails, password resets, service updates, and support responses
  • Analytics: Improve our service, understand usage patterns, and fix bugs
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, and protect our platform
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Payment Processing

We use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe handles all credit card data and is PCI DSS compliant. We do not store or have access to your full credit card numbers.

4.2 Email Services

We use Resend to send transactional emails (welcome emails, password resets, notifications). Your email address is shared with Resend solely for email delivery.

4.3 Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Umami Analytics to understand how our service is used. These services use cookies and collect anonymized usage data.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Database: PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway with encryption at rest
  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Passwords: Hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Backups: Regular automated backups with 30-day retention

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Export: Export your calculation history and account data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (transactional emails will continue)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@liendeadline.com.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management, authentication, security
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics and Umami Analytics (anonymized)

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

8. Data Retention

  • User Accounts: Retained until you request deletion
  • Calculation Logs: Retained for 1 year for history and analytics
  • API Logs: Retained for 90 days for security monitoring

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@liendeadline.com
