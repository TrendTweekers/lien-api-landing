Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email Address: When you enter your email to extend your free trial
  • Calculation Data: State, notice date, and work date you enter
  • IP Address: To prevent abuse of the free trial system
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe (we never see card numbers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide lien deadline calculations
  • To manage your free trial limits
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send account-related emails (login credentials, receipts)
  • To prevent abuse and fraud

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Railway.app servers in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We only share data with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • SendGrid/SMTP providers: For sending emails

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (email us)
  • Delete your data (email us)
  • Opt out of emails (unsubscribe link in emails)
  • Cancel your subscription anytime

6. Cookies

We use localStorage (browser storage) to track your calculation count. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: support@liendeadline.com
