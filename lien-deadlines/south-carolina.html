How lien deadlines work in South Carolina

In South Carolina, material suppliers must follow specific deadlines to protect their lien rights. Preliminary notice requirements: no mandatory preliminary notice; optional notice of furnishing recommended before final payment. The mechanics lien filing deadline is must be filed within 90 days after last furnishing. Missing either deadline results in complete loss of lien rights for that project.

Preliminary notice rules

Mechanics lien filing rules

  • Enforcement suit PLUS lis pendens required within 6 months of filing
