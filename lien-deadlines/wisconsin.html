How lien deadlines work in Wisconsin

In Wisconsin, material suppliers must follow specific deadlines to protect their lien rights. The preliminary notice deadline is required within 60 days of first furnishing. The mechanics lien filing deadline is must be recorded within 6 months (180 days) after last furnishing. Missing either deadline results in complete loss of lien rights for that project.

Preliminary notice rules

  • Notice of Intent to Lien must be served 30 days BEFORE filing
  • Required within 60 days of first furnishing
  • Subcontractor Identification Notice required for material suppliers

Mechanics lien filing rules

  • Notice of Intent to Lien must be served 30 days BEFORE filing
  • Must be recorded within 6 months (180 days) after last furnishing
  • Construction Lien must be filed within deadline
