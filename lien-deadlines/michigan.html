How lien deadlines work in Michigan

In Michigan, material suppliers must follow specific deadlines to protect their lien rights. The preliminary notice deadline is required for subcontractors/suppliers within 20 days of first furnishing. The mechanics lien filing deadline is must be filed within 90 days after last furnishing. Missing either deadline results in complete loss of lien rights for that project.

Preliminary notice rules

  • Late notice limits protection to work performed AFTER notice is served
  • Required for subcontractors/suppliers within 20 days of first furnishing
  • Notice of Furnishing required for material suppliers

Mechanics lien filing rules

  • Must serve lien on owner within 15 days of recording
  • Must be filed within 90 days after last furnishing
  • Claim of Lien must be filed within deadline
